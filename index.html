WE BELIEVE IN SERVERLESS ARCHITECTURE

Compared to cost-inhibitive traditional solutions, Serverless Architecture provides enormous platform for small and medium businesses.

Scalability, Reliability and Security are at the core of a Serverless Architecture. Either we are dealing with typical traffic or hugely fluctuating traffic, we can't go wrong with per minute billing. With third party (AWS) user authentication, database, Web Server and other services, the possibilities are unlimited. Seamless experience for local users and the ones accross the oceans can not be easier.
